About Me

Hello. Nice to meet you.

For as long as I can remember, I have been a huge nerd. Whether I was reading countless Choose Your Own Adventure puzzle books or making my own game levels using pen and paper, my fascination for riddles and creating never ceased. Envision a Gameboy-carrying 10-year old, who aspired to be a Pokemon trainer while reading the Harry Potter series for the umpteenth time. I have always believed I would follow a path where I would be able to create and problem-solve at the same time.

I like to consider my skills and knowledge as part of my ever growing "character stats". I’ve been able to work on some great projects and client work that has helped me learn the foundation of development. The amazing thing about being a developer is that your skillset is always changing and can always be improved. Only time will tell what other technologies we'll be able to use to create experiences in the future!
