About Me

Life has a way of helping you level up, as long as you embrace it.

For as long as I can remember, I have been a huge nerd. From reading countless choose your own adventure books, playing every video game I could get my hands on, to making my own levels using pen and paper. Spending hours working on brain teasers and puzzles while on the bus to school was a normal morning for me. Envision a Gameboy carrying, aspiring Pokemon trainer who’s reading the Harry Potter series for the umpteenth time. Super nerd and proud of it!

I always believed I would follow a path where I would be able to create and problem solve at the same time. Being able to create my own programs that others could use and see my ideas come to fruition was this exactly. Alas, life has a funny way of making it exciting by throwing you curveballs. I spent close to ten years working in the financial industry and moving up the corporate ladder only to feel the itch to create. It was only after going through some self reflection that I decided to jump in head first into what I am passionate about, programming.

I had taken some programming courses while in school so I knew in order to submerge myself I would need to expose myself to as much as possible. Keeping this in mind, I decided to work on becoming a Full Stack Developer. Being able to work on the front-end and be able to understand the back-end would make the most sense for me in terms of my personal growth.

Being a nerd, I like to consider my skills and knowledge as part of my ever growing character stats. I’ve been able to work on some great projects and client work that has helped me boost my stats as one would say. The awesome thing about being a developer is that your skillset is always changing and can always be improved. I’ve since learned that when life is helping you level up in your career, don’t fight it, just embrace it.
